Gelatinous Monster

Gelatinous Monster is an in-development online text-based multiplayer game. You take on the role of a freelancer persona seeking fortune and glory however you choose. Virtually immortal but physically fallible, you inhabit "sleeves" as you traverse a new life in an offworld colony.
